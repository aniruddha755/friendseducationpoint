Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: May 2, 2026

At Friends Education Point, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Basic visitor information such as browser type and device information.
  • Information you provide when contacting us.
  • Usage data to improve website performance.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve website content and user experience.
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Maintain website security and performance.

3. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and analyze website traffic.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as analytics or advertising providers. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information, but no online platform can guarantee complete security.

6. Children's Privacy

Friends Education Point does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Us page.
